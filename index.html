What is Computer Science?

Computer science is the study of computation, information, and automation. It encompasses both theoretical disciplines (such as algorithms, theory of computation, and information theory) and applied disciplines (including the design and implementation of hardware and software).

Key Areas of Computer Science

  • Algorithms and Data Structures
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Computer Architecture
  • Software Engineering
  • Human-Computer Interaction

Computer science plays a crucial role in many industries, including technology, healthcare, finance, and more. It drives innovation and helps solve complex problems through computational methods.
